Culture & Heritage

Landmarks, museums, monuments, and plazas that reflect the province's identity and enduring legacy.

In Pangasinan, history stands proudly in its museums, monuments, and plazas, each one telling a story carved from the past. From grand capitol buildings to quiet parks and striking statues, these landmarks reflect the province's identity, resilience, and artistic spirit.

Walk through these cultural spaces and discover how heritage is preserved not just in memories, but in structures that continue to inspire. Here, every monument and museum is more than a destination. It is a living reminder of Pangasinan's rich and enduring legacy.
