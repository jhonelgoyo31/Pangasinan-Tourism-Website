Pilgrimage & Faith Tours

Sacred churches, pilgrimage stops, and spiritual landmarks that reflect Pangasinan's devotion and religious heritage.

In Pangasinan, faith is deeply woven into everyday life, expressed through centuries-old churches, sacred sites, and time-honored religious traditions. From quiet chapels nestled in towns to grand basilicas that welcome thousands of pilgrims, each place reflects the province's enduring devotion and spiritual heritage.

Embark on a journey of reflection and renewal as you visit revered destinations like the Minor Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary of Manaoag, along with other historic churches and pilgrimage routes across the province. Here, every step becomes an opportunity to pause, pray, and reconnect with faith.

Discover Pangasinan not just as a place to explore, but as a sanctuary for the soul, where tradition, devotion, and community come together in a meaningful spiritual experience.
