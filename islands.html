Islands & Beaches

Pristine coastlines, island escapes, and shorelines that reveal Pangasinan's natural beauty and coastal charm.

In Pangasinan, nature reveals its beauty through pristine islands and sun-kissed shores, each offering a peaceful escape shaped by the sea. From crystal-clear waters to unique rock formations, these coastal gems reflect the province's natural charm and coastal heritage.

Step onto these shores and experience a different rhythm of life, where waves, wind, and wide horizons create moments of calm and wonder. Here, every island and beach is more than a destination. It is a gateway to Pangasinan's breathtaking coastal story.
